Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/4 cup Greek Yogurt
  • A splash of milk
  • Blueberries
  • 1 tbsp Chia seeds

Steps

  1. Place the oats and chia seeds in a container
  2. Add the yogurt and mix well
  3. Add a splash of milk if you feel the consistency is too thick
  4. Mix well
  5. Leave in refrigerator overnight
  6. Serve with blueberries or any fruit of your choice
