Daal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red split lentils
  • 5 cups water
  • salt
  • Black pepper
  • Coriander powder
  • Red chilli powder

Steps

  1. Wash the lentils in cold water
  2. Bring 5 cups of water to the boil
  3. Add the spices, stir and add the daal to the water
  4. Place a lid on the saucepan and allow to simmer, stirring regularly
  5. Once the daal has thickened to your preference, remove from heat
  6. Serve with boiled rice or roti
