Chicken Curry

Here's a traditional, easy and delicous recipe for making a chicken curry with a smooth tomato and onion base and a lovely kick with the addition of chilli powder.

Ingredients

  • Bone in chicken cut into pieces
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Red Chilli powder and salt

Steps

  1. Fry onions for 2 minutes, then add garlic
  2. Add salt and chilli
  3. Add tinned tomatoes and cook for 4 minutes
  4. Add the chicken and mix thoroughly
  5. Add 2 cups of water and simmer for 25 minutes
  6. Check the chicken is cooked through and serve immediately with rice or naan
